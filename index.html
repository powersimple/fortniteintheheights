About Fortnite in the Heights

Silicon Harlem, iHeartMedia, and The Armory Foundation are proud to bring Fortnite in the Heights to New York City. The event will take place at the iconic Armory Track Arena in Washington Heights. This family friendly event is designed to combine gaming, technology, and health throughout the experience. We will be streaming from the event

Gamers

This is a BYOD (bring your own device) tournament day. Fortnite can be played on mobile devices, tablets, and most other platforms. You will need to check back often to get all the details of the rules and code of conduct information. Anyone can win, play your best because we are donating some of the proceeds to the Armory Foundation. Be sure to help spread the word using the #uptownfortnite and connect with us on all the social media outlets.
